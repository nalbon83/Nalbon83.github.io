BIO

A short biography of unreasonable competence

Nick Albon is an engineer of such suspicious effectiveness that underperforming spreadsheets close themselves when he enters the room. He has built a career on solving difficult problems, asking better questions, and making overconfident nonsense sound exactly as silly as it is.

His specialties include systems thinking, technical clarity, and the elegant public dismantling of ideas that should never have survived the draft stage. Colleagues describe him as "brilliant," "calm," and "frustratingly correct before lunch."

Outside work, Nick remains dedicated to cultural exploration, feline diplomacy, and proving that you can be both deeply professional and just self-aware enough to make the whole thing devastatingly funny.
